You may be interested to know that S.I.S. is a member of S.A.W.D.A.C. - The Siding and Window Dealers Association of Canada. S.A.W.D.A.C. provides education for consumers on what to look for when hiring a contractor as well as educating contractors on the demanding procedures and high standards they must follow in order to earn and retain their nationally recognized S.A.W.D.A.C. certification. The Siding and Window Dealers Association of Canada is an assurance that member dealers and contractors will deliver consistently high standards of products, installations and business ethics. S.I.S. has met the SAWDAC membership criteria, committed to their code of ethics and provides a five-year workmanship guarantee.

S.I.S. is also a member, after meeting specified requirements,  of the Siding Contractors Association of Alberta (SCAA).  The SCAA offers professional standards that must be adhered to, ongoing learning about new innovations in the siding industry as well as strengthening the relationship with safety and code officials, government officials and the consumer.

You can count on our commitment to quality.  Often times our crews come across decayed and decomposed wood materials (also known as wood rot) when removing old siding, stucco and windows from Calgary homes. When this happens, you can rest assured that we will remove all of the rotten wood framing, sheathing and other compromised materials such as mouldy insulation.   We will then treat the area to kill any remaining spores, if need be, and then rebuild and reframe the walls and seal up your building envelope so that water will not be able to infiltrate into your home again.  We have the knowledge and expertise to remediate this type of situation.
