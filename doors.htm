Portatec Premium Doors

Innovation, aesthetics and durability are the attributes that define a Portatec Door.  Portatec has been designing and manufacturing Canadian made top-quality exterior doors in its own manufacturing facilities for over 25 years.  They can be built to meet not only your needs, but also to reflect your own particular taste. Below are just some of the vast array of options available:

  • Overall superior quality

  • Door systems that are 100% prefinished - no painting or staining required

  • The largest glass selection of any door manufacturer in Canada

  • Raised Nobless door mouldings (as opposed to an embossed door)

  • Vinyl edge cap on all edges of the door for enhanced durability

  • 22-Gauge hot galvanized steel, which is stronger than most others found on the market

  • Real Wood Veneers with various wood species and stains available

Endless combinations of doorlites, sidelites, transoms as well as venting sidelites available

Portatec Collection - Steel entry door Systems


This high end steel door will provide you with a strong structure, a meticulous finish, enhance security and superior durability.  This door being almost maintenance free, is assembled with two galvanized steel sheets over a strong wood structure.  The central core is injected with polyurethane foam which provides superior thermal insulation.  The Portatec Collection steel door will also provide you with:

  • 22-Gauge hot galvanized steel

  • Ultra resistant paint

  • 25 Standard Colours to choose from

  • Custom colours also available

  • Security plate in the door frame, next to the dead bolt, for increased protection against break-ins

  • Vinyl edge cap on all edges of the door

  • Glass moulding in extruded aluminum which will not warp when painted a dark colour and exposed to sunlight.

  • The option of an elegant Raised Nobless door moulding - unavailable from any other Canadian door manufacturer.

Portatec Wood Finish Door Systems


 Portatec Wood Finish doors are assembled using an exclusive process and created with the experience of qualified personnel.  The wood veneer finish is applied onto a steel door and is remarkably elegant and allows our customers to achieve the perfect balance of durability and refinement.  There are three options to choose from:

  1. An interior wood finish with steel exterior

  2. An exterior wood finish with steel interior

  3. The Absolute Wood finish or a wood finish both inside and out

The wood finish on steel doors is available in 10 standard colours with the choice of six different wood species (oak, birch, pine, mahogany, cherry and cedar).  The stain can also be made to match any colour that you may require to match existing finishes in your home.  Many of the same options available on the steel entry doors are also availaible on the wood finish including:

  • 22-Gauge hot galvanized steel

  • Security plate in the door frame, next to the dead bolt, for increased protection against break-insVinyl edge cap on all edges of the door

  • Glass moulding in extruded aluminum which will not warp when painted a dark colour and exposed to sunlight.

  • The option of an elegant Raised Nobless door moulding - unavailable from any other Canadian door manufacturer.

North Star Door Collections

North Star Fiberglass Doors - Textured (Wood) Skin

North Star Terrace Doors

North Star Terrace doors (you may know them as being Garden doors) are a secure door for your deck or patio entrance.  One side operates as a regular entrance door whereas the other side operates like a crank out casement window.  This gives you the best of both worlds - security when both sides are locked as well as the option for exceptional ventilation when needed.
